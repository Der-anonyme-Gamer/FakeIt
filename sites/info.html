About FakeIt

How to Play
1
Role Assignment

At the start of the game, each player is secretly assigned a role - either an Impostor or a Crewmate.

2
Game Rounds

Crewmates complete tasks to win, while Impostors try to eliminate Crewmates without being caught.

3
Meetings

When a body is reported or an emergency meeting is called, players discuss to find the Impostor.

4
Voting

Players vote to eject who they believe is the Impostor. Majority vote decides who gets ejected.

5
Win Conditions

Crewmates win by completing all tasks or ejecting all Impostors. Impostors win by eliminating enough Crewmates.
